Hello world,

I am a young engineer from Ecole des Ponts ParisTech, passionate about technology and entrepreneurship. I have recently finished a Master of Management of Innovation at the University of Toronto, to better understand the business side of technology.

Among other experiences, I have worked as a financial analyst at Natixis and EY, and in business development at Xtreme Labs, a mobile development startup (acquired by Pivotal in 2013). I am currently working as a consultant at Capgemini Consulting on challenges of digital transformation and Big Data.

Among my side projects, I have collaborated with exciting technology startups such as:

  • My Major Company, the leading crowdfunding platform in France,
  • Solar Ship, a solar aircraft manufacturer planning to revolutionize transportation
  • My Studio Assistant, a web publishing service for artists, I am also the co-founder of Refresh, a crowdsourcing platform for students at Ecole des Ponts.

I am always learning new things, from economics to coding. I enjoy mathematical models, algorithms and analyzing data. In my spare time, you can find me on the Internet, reading and blogging about technology and innovation.
